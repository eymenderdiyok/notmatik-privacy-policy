Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the NotMatik mobile application.

NotMatik is an educational utility app that allows students to enter their exam scores and automatically calculates term or year-end grade averages. The app also indicates whether the student qualifies for a Certificate of Achievement or Certificate of Appreciation based on the calculated results.

NotMatik does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All entered grades are processed locally on the user’s device only. No information is uploaded to external servers or shared with third parties.

The application does not use analytics services, advertising SDKs, tracking technologies, or third-party data processors.
