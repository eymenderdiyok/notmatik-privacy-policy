NotMatik

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Overview

NotMatik (“the App”) is a privacy-focused educational application designed to help students organize academic information and calculate grade averages. The App is designed to function entirely offline and does not require account creation, sign-in, or identity verification.

2. Data Collected by the App

The App may store data entered directly by the user, including but not limited to:

• Course names and class levels
• Exam scores, oral grades, and activity grades
• Grade calculation preferences and weighting settings
• Application preferences such as selected language

This data is considered user-generated educational content and is used solely to provide the App’s core functionality.

3. How Data Is Stored

All data is stored locally on the user’s device using Apple’s on-device storage technologies. Academic data is stored using Apple’s SwiftData framework, while application preferences are stored using UserDefaults (AppStorage).

No data is transmitted off the device.

4. Data Usage

Collected data is used exclusively for app functionality, including grade calculation, result presentation, and personalization of user preferences.

The data is not used for advertising, analytics, tracking, profiling, or marketing purposes.

5. Data Sharing

NotMatik does not share data with third parties. No data is sold, transferred, or disclosed to external services.

6. Tracking and Analytics

The App does not use analytics frameworks, advertising SDKs, tracking technologies, cookies, or similar mechanisms. Users are not tracked across apps or websites.

7. Network and Cloud Services

NotMatik does not use cloud synchronization services such as iCloud, does not perform background network requests, and does not require an internet connection to function.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

All data remains on the user’s device until the user chooses to delete it. Data can be removed by deleting entries within the App or by uninstalling the App.

9. Children’s Privacy

NotMatik does not knowingly collect personal identifiable information such as names, email addresses, or contact details. No accounts or online services are used.

10. Security

The App relies on Apple’s platform security features, including application sandboxing and system-level data protection. No additional permissions are requested beyond what is required for basic functionality.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.
